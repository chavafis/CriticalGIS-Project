About this site

This repository now uses a visual-essay style inspired by a modern long-form web template. It combines narrative structure with an interactive map, a clickable city list, and historical population estimates for Jewish communities in Mediterranean port cities.

The map and list are designed to work together: click any city in the list to fly to that location on the map and reveal the population details.

Interactive city list

Tap any city to center the map and open its popup. The estimates are drawn from historical population data for the late 19th and early 20th centuries.
